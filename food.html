Find Free Food Near You

Hope Haven’s food finder helps you locate free food programs such as food pantries, community meals, kids’ meals, and mobile food trucks.

  • Search by city or ZIP code.
  • See locations on the map and in the list.
  • Check live availability with “Meals served today” status.

How the CT Food Finder Works

Type your city or ZIP code into the search box on the right. The map will move to matching locations and show markers for each free food program in that area.

Under the map, you will see a list of programs with their address, hours, and a live status showing whether meals are served today.
