Find Emergency Shelter

Use this page to search for sample emergency shelters in Connecticut. In a full project, this map could connect to ShelterLink for live bed updates from partner shelters.

  • Search by city name or ZIP code.
  • See shelters on the map with available beds.
  • Check “Beds available today” in the results list.

How to Use This Shelter Map

Type a city (for example, New Haven) or ZIP code into the search box on the right, then click Search. The map will move to that area and show markers for emergency shelters in our sample data.

Click a marker to see the shelter name, city, and how many beds are available out of the total for tonight. The list under the map also shows these details.

Need Immediate Help?

Call 2-1-1 Connecticut if you need urgent help finding a safe place to stay tonight. A specialist can connect you to real shelters and support services near you.
