About Hope Haven

Hope Haven brings together shelters, food programs, volunteers, and community partners to support neighbors facing hardship across Connecticut.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safety, dignity, and essential support for individuals and families experiencing homelessness or crisis.

Our Vision

We envision a Connecticut where no one has to sleep outside or go hungry, and where every community has simple tools to share resources quickly.

What We Do

Hope Haven is a collaborative project created to connect shelters, food programs, volunteers, and local organizations in one shared platform.

Emergency Shelter Support

Helping individuals find available shelter beds and safe housing options.

Food Assistance

Connecting families with food pantries, hot meals, and grocery programs.

Volunteer Connections

Linking volunteers with shelters and food programs that need support.

ShelterLink Innovation

A real-time system concept to help shelters update bed availability instantly.

Our Story

Hope Haven began with a question: How can we make it easier for people in crisis to find help quickly?

Students and community partners collaborated to design a platform where shelters, food programs, and volunteers could work together to support individuals in need.

How You Can Help

You can support Hope Haven in many ways:

  • Volunteer at a local shelter or food program
  • Donate supplies, food, or funds
  • Share Hope Haven with community organizations
