BECOME A VOLUNTEER

When you volunteer with Hope Haven, you are supporting neighbors across Connecticut who need safe shelter, food, and guidance. Volunteers are a key part of our work, whether you join as an individual, a family, or a community group.

Below are some ways you can serve:

SHELTER SUPPORT

  1. Greet guests and help them find check-in and basic services.
  2. Serve meals or snacks during busy evening and weekend hours.
  3. Sort blankets, clothing, and hygiene kits for distribution.

FOOD & MEAL PROGRAMS

  1. Pack food boxes for families and outreach teams.
  2. Assist at community meals and food-pickup locations.
  3. Help load donations for partner shelters and food pantries.

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

  1. Share Hope Haven resource information with local partners.
  2. Support supply drives, awareness events, or seasonal projects.
  3. Help with administrative and small organizational tasks.
